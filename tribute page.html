BIOGRAPHY

Mahendra Singh Dhoni,known as"captain cool," is a highly revered cricketer who led the national team to glory in the 2011 world cup. Born in Ranchi,Bihar (now Jharkhand) on July 7, 1981, Dhoni's journey from a small-town boy to an international icon is a story of dedication and talent. He is a wicketkeeper-batsman with a unique style and a claim demeanor under pressure.
